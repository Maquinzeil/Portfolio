Social Media Marketing (SMM) Workflow

1. Set Your Goals 🎯

Define what you want to achieve (followers, engagement, sales). Make goals SMART: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound.

2. Know Your Audience 👥

Understand who you're targeting: age, interests, behavior. Analyze competitors' audiences and engagement patterns.

3. Pick Your Platforms 📱

Focus on the best channels for your audience (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook). Start with 1–2 platforms.

4. Create a Content Plan 🗓️

Choose weekly themes or content pillars. Use planning tools like Google Sheets, Notion, or Trello to map it out.

5. Design Your Content 🎨

Use tools like Canva or CapCut. Stick to your brand's style: fonts, colors, logos.

6. Write Captions + Hashtags ✍️

Use engaging captions: Hook + Value + CTA. Add 10–15 strategic hashtags per post.

7. Schedule Posts 📤

Batch and schedule using Meta Business Suite or Buffer. Pick optimal times for publishing.

8. Engage Daily 💬

Respond to comments and DMs, engage with other accounts, and post Stories or polls regularly.

9. Track Performance 📊

Use Instagram Insights or Metricool to monitor reach, engagement, and follower growth.

10. Adjust & Improve 🔄

Focus on content that works best. Update your strategy monthly and test new formats.
